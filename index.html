What is School Savings™?

School Savings™ is a social enterprise that teaches children to save by accepting their saving deposits AT SCHOOL. It is also a processor for the Federal Reserve Bank and has been evaluated and approved by the U.S. Department of Education for public and private schools.

Its goal is to re-instill the savings ethic in youth. A secure, cloud-based deposit system collects student (and faculty) deposits, manages saving operations and credits the accounts DIRECTLY through the Federal Reserve Bank.

School Savings includes an Online Savings Register and an animated Budgeting app that helps students develop money management skills. An integrated student incentives system keeps students motivated to save FREQUENTLY.

School Savings is a powerful tool that enables financial institutions and schools to develop meaningful relationships with families and increase financial literacy in their communities.
