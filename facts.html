BENEFITS

  • School Savings creates new relationships with local families. Seventy-three (73) percent of School Saver households are new to the sponsor financial institution. Cross-over sales to parents average 4.63 products with checking, savings accounts and loans leading the way.

  • Parent Volunteers save money by collecting and dropping students funds off at the branch.
  • School Savings is a “mortgage incubator.” Growing families need larger homes. More than twenty (20) percent of School Saver parents acquiring a mortgage selected their School Savings sponsor for their loan.

  • Hand posting is eliminated because School Savings processes student deposits directly through the Federal Reserve Bank via its iDebit® technology.

  • Financial institutions receive Community Reinvestment Act (CRA) credit for School Savings programs in low income schools when renewing their license with the FDIC.

  • School Savings’™ U.S. Department of Education approval makes acceptance by schools automatic. School Savings is the ONLY USDOE-approved school banking program.
